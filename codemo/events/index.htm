Tuesday Social Gathering

Every Tuesday evening Codemo members come together at 1 Osbourne Ave from around 6pm till about 10 or 11pm. What do we get up to? Well, a typical Tuesday will include talking, eating, music, dancing, a pool table, and a lot of laughter. Sometimes some of us will play other games like domino and bingo. Sometimes someone from outside comes and gives a talk for about an hour on a topic of use to members, such as health services. Overall, Codemo's Tuesday evening gatherings provide a very friendly, warm atmosphere in which to unwind and be together. We'll see you there! Phone Nelson on 8502 6300 for more information.

English Conversation Class

Each Saturday evening from 6 pm Codemo offers a free, informal, and fun English conversation class. Initially taught by Dan Palmer, Big Carlo is currentlyleading the class. See a picture of the class in action on August 23, 2005.

Permaculture Workshops

One of CODEMO's aims is to learn and promote permaculture, part of which means learning how to grow more of your own food at home. We have a workshop at the house of a different member every few weeks. See photos of the day we had making over Vilma's garden here!.

The next permablitz day is Sunday Semtember 23rd from 12 noon at the house of Nelson Campus (14 Whiteside St Springvale 3171). Call 0433559928 or 8502 6300 for more information.

Martes; Reuniones sociales

cada martes por la noche los miembros de codemo se reunen a eso de las 6pm hasta las 10 o 11 de la noche en donde cada uno pone de su parte creando muy buena atmosfera por que nos encanta estar juntos respetando a cada uno.Cada martes es diferente,pero por lo general hay comida,charlas,mesa de billar,musica,baile,bingo,celebraciones y alguien dispuesto aensenarte alguna actividad como ganchillo ,molde,castellano,baile....Todos aprecian nuevas ideas!Esperamos verte ahi!

Clase de Ingles
