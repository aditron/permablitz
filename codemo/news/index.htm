Codemo Attends PERMABLITZ #3 at Grandma Monica's House in Moonee Ponds! (21/5/06)

With another great turnout we all had a lovely day learning together at Monica's house. Along with putting in a new keyhole path garden bed, planting broad beans, and a house tour with magnificent Monica, we learned about growing your own sprouts, cheese making, peanut butter making, wheatgrass juicing, and fruit and vegetable juicing. There was lots of deliciious food to share and a number of new faces came from the local area - we hope they will continue to meet and organise similar days in future!See photos here . Codemo Attends the Thomas Street Open Day in Full Force! (7/5/06)

With around 150-200 people attending the Thomas Street Open Day, Codemo was ready and waiting with empanadas, pupusas, tacos, chilean hotdogs, and a raffle for wine and a potplant. Codemo raised almost $300 towards the cause of saving the Thomas Street garden and a great day was had by all with garden tours, talks, poetry, and of course great South American food!See photos here .

Codemo's Second Permaculture Day even Huger Success! (23/4/06)

With around 30 folks coming along at Nelson's casa in Springvale for a truly wonderful afternoon of seed-planting, cutting progating, chook-tractoring, hot composting, fine dining, and permasalsaing. We had people from El Salvador, Chile, Argentina, Cuba, Peru, Romania, Ireland, Singapore and even Australia! Great communal atmosphere, much new learning, plenty of seeds and plants off to new homes, and many connections made, plans hatched and phone numbers swapped. Bravo!See photos here . Our next permaculture day is on May 7 at 16 Thomas Street in Clayton from midday till 5pm - see more details here.
