After ten years of permablitzin', it's time to party!
Now that Permabitz has been eating the suburbs for ten years, we're having a party to celebrate - and we'd love it if you could join us!

Join us for an afternoon in the sun as we thank the founders, the hosts, the fabulous designers, the Collective members from both then and now, those that inspired us - and of course the volunteers that make it all possible.

There'll be food (you like pizza right?), there'll be drinks, there'll be live music from The Orbweavers and there'll even be incredibly fun games - including chicken bingo!

And it wouldn't be a Permablitz event if we didn't have workshops - check out all the things that are happening in the list below!

It's all happening at Peppertree Place this Sunday from 11am. We can't wait to see you there!
